# DoDo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2025

## Introduction

DoDo Operations (hereinafter referred to as "Company") considers protecting Users' (hereinafter referred to as "Users") personal information in the mobile application "DoDo" (hereinafter referred to as "Service") an important responsibility. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use and protect it.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information**: Email address, password, username
- **Profile Information**: Name, date of birth, profile picture (optional)
- **Inquiry Information**: Contents of inquiries, opinions, and requests
- **Usage Data**: Text, questions, and feedback entered within the Service

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device type, OS, language settings, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Statistics**: App usage frequency, feature usage, error logs
- **Log Information**: Access date and time, IP address, referrer
- **Location Information**: Approximate location (only when permitted)

### 1.3 Information Obtained from Third Parties

- Basic profile information provided by social login services when used

## 2. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Provision and Operation**: Providing, maintaining, and improving the Service
2. **Customer Support**: Responding to inquiries and providing support
3. **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage and developing new features
4. **Security**: Preventing unauthorized use and ensuring security
5. **Notifications**: Delivering important notices and service updates
6. **Marketing**: Announcing campaigns and new features (with consent)
7. **Legal Compliance**: Fulfilling legal obligations and exercising legal rights

## 3. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose Users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

### 3.1 With User Consent

When prior consent has been obtained from the User

### 3.2 Disclosure to Service Providers

When outsourcing operations necessary for service provision (under appropriate supervision):
- Cloud service providers
- Payment processing services
- Customer support services
- Analytics services

### 3.3 As Required by Law

- When disclosure is required by law
- When necessary to protect life, body, or property
- When necessary to improve public health or promote the healthy development of children
- When cooperation with government agencies is necessary for the execution of legally prescribed duties

### 3.4 Business Transfers

When business is transferred due to merger, company split, business transfer, or other reasons

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Storage Location

Users' personal information is stored in Japan or on secure cloud servers designated by the Company.

### 4.2 Retention Period

- **Account Information**: While the account is active, and 30 days after account deletion
- **Usage Logs**: Up to 1 year
- **Inquiry Information**: 3 years after resolution

### 4.3 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:
- Data encryption (in transit and at rest)
- Access controls
- Regular security audits
- Employee training

## 5. User Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their personal information:

### 5.1 Right to Access

You may request disclosure of your personal information held by the Company.

### 5.2 Right to Rectification, Addition, and Deletion

You may request correction, addition, or deletion if your personal information is inaccurate.

### 5.3 Right to Suspension and Erasure

You may request suspension of use or erasure of your personal information.

### 5.4 Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from the settings screen within the app. Upon account deletion, your personal information will be removed from our systems (except where retention is required by law).

### 5.5 Data Portability

You may export your data in a structured format.

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise the above rights, please contact us at the inquiry address below. We may request additional information for identity verification.

## 6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

### 6.1 Use of Cookies

The Service (especially the web version) uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

- **Essential Cookies**: Providing basic service functionality
- **Functional Cookies**: Saving user preferences
- **Analytics Cookies**: Analyzing usage and improving the service
- **Advertising Cookies**: Delivering personalized ads (disabled on paid plans)

### 6.2 Managing Cookies

Users may refuse cookies through browser settings. However, some features may become unavailable.

## 7. Privacy of Minors

The Service is intended for users aged 13 and older. Those under 13 are not permitted to use the Service. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a user under 13, we will promptly delete such information.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the Service or by email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified Privacy Policy.
